Ultimate Frisbee

Welcome to The Flying Snatch's Ultimate Frisbee webpage! Are you interested in ultimate frisbee? Do you know what ultimate frisbee is? Well if you're interested in ultimate, or don't even know anything about it, then you've come to the right place. Here you'll learn about what ultimate frisbee is about and how to play it. We encourage everyone, to come out and play. New, kinda new, or experienced players are always welcome at practice at any point of the season. Our practices are on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m. on the field inside the UNCA track. We'll teach you everything you need to know about the sport. If you do come to practice please bring, if you can, cleats or running shoes, lots of water, a white shirt and a dark shirt. So take a look around the webpage and hope you enjoy and learn a little something about ultimate. Hope to see you on the fields.
